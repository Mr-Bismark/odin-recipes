Chicken Fried Rice

Description

This Chicken Fried Rice is a savory, satisfying meal that combines tender chicken, vegetables, and fluffy rice all stir-fried in a flavorful sauce.

It's perfect for using leftover rice and comes together in no time.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • Diced chicken breast
  • Eggs
  • Carrots and peasr
  • Green onions
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook diced chicken until browned; set aside.
  2. Scramble eggs in the same pan and remove.
  3. Stir-fry veggies and garlic in sesame oil.
  4. Add rice, then soy sauce, and stir well.
  5. Mix in chicken and scrambled eggs.
  6. Scoop onto baking sheet and bake 10-12 minutes.
  7. Garnish with green onions and serve hot.

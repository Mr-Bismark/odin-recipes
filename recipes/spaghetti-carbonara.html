Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's rich, creamy, and surprisingly easy to make with just a few ingredients.

Perfect for a quick dinner that tastes like it came from a restaurant!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Eggs
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Pancetta or bacon
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Cook pancetta until crispy in a pan.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs with Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain pasta and reserve some water.
  5. Mix hot pasta with pancetta, then stir in egg mixture.
  6. Add reserved water if needed to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.

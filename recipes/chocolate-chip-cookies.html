Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These chocolate chip cookies are soft, chewy, and loaded with gooey chocolate chips. They're a crowd-pleaser and easy to bake from scratch with pantry staples.

Perfect with a glass of milk or as a sweet snack!

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Unsalted butter
  • Brown sugar
  • White sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl.
  3. Cream butter and sugars until fluffy.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla to wet mixture.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients, then fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop onto baking sheet and bake 10-12 minutes.
  7. Let cool and enjoy!
